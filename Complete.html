The Base

A Filming Camp

An experience like none other!
Have you ever dreamed of being in a film?
Well, here's your chance!

The Base is an all-girls filming camp!

  • Dates: All summer long!
  • Time: During the day and some nights
  • Place: Where we need to film
  • Directed By: Meira Levi

The Base is a creative outlet for girls who enjoy the performing arts. Over the course of the summer, the girls put together a full-length film. They learn about the art of movie-making, as well as acting and filming techniques. With loads of fun, friendships, and dedicated work, the girls put together a masterpiece!
